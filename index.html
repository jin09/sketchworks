Our Services

GLAZING FABRICATION DRAWINGS

Fabrication drawings plays an important role in every project. SW team has in depth knowledge of fabrication drawings as few of the team members have on hands experience in US projects.

This helps our team to bring accuracy and reliability during the production of fab drawings.

Project Management

SW can provide project management services to the glazing contractors in US right from the Bid stage to the closeout of the project. Our Project Managers have an experience of over 4+ years in the glazing industry in California, US. We know how important the submittals, shop drawings, material ordering & fabrication drawings in a project.

Storefront & CW Shop Drawings

Our in house engineer has over 25 years of glazing experience in US and is capable of providing design assistance on any project type. We are capable of doing Alum Storefront,Curtain Wall, Skylights,Glass Doors,Bi Folding shop drawings and very well aware of DSA requirements. We provide 2 separate shop drawings for DSA & Non DSA submission.

3D Modeling & BIM

SW offers wide range of 3D & BIM modeling services. We partner with architects,

contractors and owners to convert their concept into a beautiful 3D rendering. We can covert an existing 2D drawings ( paper & electronic format ) into a 3D model.

Architectural Design

SW is specialized in providing detailed CAD drawings. Our team focuses on owner's design intent & project budget and ensures minimal errors on the project drawings.Every Project is led by a senior architect and supported by our in house quality assurance team to ensure that the final output is accurate and reliable.

Quantity Takeoff & Bid Packages

SW offers all services related to Quantity takeoffs, estimates & complete bid packages for both sub contractors & General contractors.

Our team is aware of all bidding requirements and can provide very accurate takeoffs/ Bid packages.
